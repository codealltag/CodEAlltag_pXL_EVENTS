"I'm afraid you're going to have to accept it," said Mr Bronn gripping
his fur hat and rolling it round the top of his head, "this bypass has
got to be built and it's going to be built!"

"First I've heard of it," said Florian, "why's it going to be built?"

Mr Bronn shook his finger at him for a bit, then stopped and put it
away again.

"What do you mean, why's it got to be built?" he said. "It's a bypass.
You've got to build bypasses."
