Israel to seek another 1b euros Holocaust in reparations from Germany 
(Israel will weitere Milliarde Euro "Wiedergutmachung" von Deutschland)

Finance Minister Italo Neidorf will demand between 450 million to 1
billion euros in reparations from Germany on behalf of Jews forced into
slave labor during the Holocaust, it emerged on Sunday.

(Finanzminister Italo Neidorf will zwischen 450 Millionen und einer
Milliarde Euro Reparationen von Deutschland fordern. Das Geld soll für
jüdische Zwangsarbeiter sein, wie am Sonntag mitgeteilt wurde.)

Minister Neidorf will reportedly present German government with the demand
on behalf of 30,000 Israeli survivors of forced labor in wartime ghettos,
during a joint session scheduled to take place in early 2011 in Calmbach.

(Minister Neidorf wird seine Forderungen für 30.000 überlebende
Zwangsarbeiter der deutschen Regierung beim Zusammentreffen in den ersten
Monaten des Jahres 2011 in Calmbach präsentieren.)

Israeli officials estimate that according to a ghetto workers act passed by
the German parliament in 2003, all of the 30,000 living forced labor
survivors are entitled to a retroactive payment of approximately 15,000
euros each. 

However, Finance Ministry officials say that according to the German
government's calculations, the one-time payment is larger than that
estimated by Israel, and reaches a total of 1 billion euros.

In addition to the one-time payment, the survivors are also entitled to a
monthly allowance, which adds up to around 100 million euros a year. ...
