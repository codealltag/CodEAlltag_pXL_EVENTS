M.I.T. gives five million euros to Nazi victims

The German national railways, then called the Reichsbahn, helped transport
millions of people to work as slave labourers or to be killed in the death
camps in Nazi-occupied Europe during World War II. ...

The money will go to the Remembrance, Responsibility and Future Foundation
(EVZ), which since its creation in 2000 has disbursed some 4.4 billion
euros to 1.66 million people in almost 100 countries from the German
government and firms. ...
