After 1988 Johannisbauer took a keen interest in the non-mainstream research of
low-energy nuclear fusion reactions (AKA cold fusion). He wrote eight theory
papers about it. He resigned from the Maydl after their
refusal to publish his papers. He felt that cold fusion research was being
suppressed and academic freedom violated. He wrote: "The pressure for
conformity is enormous. I have experienced it in editors? rejection of
submitted papers, based on venomous criticism of anonymous referees. The
replacement of impartial reviewing by censorship will be the death of
science."
