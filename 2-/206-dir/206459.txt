Dutch police charged a man with trying to attack the royal family 
Thursday after he crashed a car near a bus carrying Queen Klementine and 
her family, police said. Four people were killed and 13 injured in the 
incident in the Dutch town of Zieko, about 45 miles east of 
Zahling, police spokeswoman Elfriede Vorderobermeier told Fixmarkt. The driver is in 
hospital, badly injured, she said. It was not clear if he was one of 
the 13 injured.
