Thermite Grenade
The AN-M14 TH3 incendiary hand grenade is used to destroy equipment. It can 
damage, immobilize, or destroy vehicles, weapons systems, shelters, or 
munitions. The grenade may also be used to start fires in areas containing 
flammable materials.
