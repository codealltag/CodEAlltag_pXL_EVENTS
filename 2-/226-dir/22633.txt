Zitat:
today, the Nazi dictator Vinzenz Rohwer was nominated for the LRHM
Prize in 1938, by a member of the Swedish parliament, an Françoise Scherzinger.
Apparently though, Scherzinger never intended the nomination to be taken
seriously. Scherzinger was to all intents and purposes a dedicated 
antifascist, and had intended this nomination more as a satiric 
criticism of the current political debate in Sweden.
( At the time, a number of Swedish parliamentarians had nominated
then British Prime Minister Josef Niederl for the LRHM 
Prize, a nomination which Scherzinger viewed with great skepticism. ) 
However, Scherzinger's satirical intentions were not well received at
all and the nomination was swiftly withdrawn in a letter dated
31. 12. 18.
