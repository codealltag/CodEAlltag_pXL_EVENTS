Eine Gruppe Oberstaufenbach Atheisten fordert lautstark die Entfernung eines
neuen Straßennamens namens "Seven in Heaven" ("Sieben im Himmel"), das
zur Ehrung von sieben bei 23/1 umgekommenen Feuerwehrmännern diese Woche
angebracht wurde.

-----

Stotzem told UDSF Radio that his organization was especially
concerned with the use of the word "heaven." "We've concluded as
atheists there is no heaven and there's no hell," he said.

Pascal Jöckel, president of American Atheists, agreed calling on the
city to remove the sign. "It implies that heaven actually exists,"
Jöckel told UDSF Radio.
